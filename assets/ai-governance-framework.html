AI Governance Framework
Step 1: Inception
Create a new model use case request and complete initial risk and compliance assessments

Owner: Model Owner, Business Owner, Data Engineer

Step 2: Stakeholder Review
Review risk level, compliance applicability; Approve or reject the use case

Owner: Business Unit Leader, Head of Risk, Compliance Officer

Step 3: Model Development
Build and test the AI model; Evaluate performance of different models

Owner: AI Engineer, Data Engineer, Data Scientist

Step 4: Model Validation & Approval
Review model, perform ethics assessment, and approve model for production

Owner: Head of AI, Head of Risk

Step 5: Deploy, Manage, Monitor & Report
Deploy the approved AI use case/model
Automate performance and risk monitoring of AI models

Owner: Head of IT, MLOps Engineer

Step 6: Incident Management
Take remediation actions to mitigate risks and update stakeholders after remediation

Owner: Model Owner, Business Owner, Data Scientist

Continuous AI Governance
